Description

"Enjoy pizzeria-quality taste right from your own kitchen in a fraction of the time. This simple, crowd-pleasing recipe features a golden, crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite classic toppings. It is the ultimate quick, comforting weeknight dinner that proves homemade is always better."

Ingredients

  • 1 16 ounce ball of pizza dough, 12 to 16 ounces is fine
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, for rubbing dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • ▢3 ounces thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, about 1 and 1/4 cups
  • ▢2.5 ounces pepperoni , about half a 5 oz package
  • ▢2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Create your pizza dough
  2. Place a pizza stone
  3. Preheat your oven to 550 degrees F
  4. Prepare a work surface.
  5. Roll out the dough
  6. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper
  7. Par bake
  8. Top your pizza
  9. Bake the pizza
  10. Remove the pizza
