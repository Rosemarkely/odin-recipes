Description

Sink your teeth into the Ultimate Ultimate Smash Burger: a diner-style masterpiece featuring ultra-crispy, lacy edges, a deeply caramelized crust, and a hit of savory umami that blows standard backyard burgers away.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; Mix.
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill; Cook 6-8 per side
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
